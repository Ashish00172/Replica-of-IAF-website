AIR CHIEF MARSHAL AP SINGH

PVSM AVSM

CHIEF OF THE
AIR STAFF

Air Chief Marshal AP Singh PVSM AVSM was commissioned into the fighter stream of IAF on 21 Dec 1984. He is an alumnus of National Defence Academy, Defence Services Staff College and National Defence College. He is a Qualified Flying Instructor and an Experimental Test Pilot with more than 5000 hours of service flying on a variety of fixed and rotary wing aircraft.
